About Me

I'm Jeff Goens and I live in Lexington, Kentucky. After working 32 years in the Automotive Industry, 27 of those at Toyota Motor Manufacturing, I decided to pursue a new career path as a Web Developer. With no prior web developing experience, I started taking classes several different ways. Some through online courses such as Harvard's CS50 class with edX, and Codecademy for JavaScript and SQL.

For in-person instruction, I enrolled in the Awesome Inc Intro to Web Developmnent class which concentrated on the Front End technologies HTML, CSS and JavaScript. After completing the Intro class, I decided to enroll in their Bootcamp. After completing 8 weeks, I've furthered my understanding of the Front End, while additionally learning Git, Bootstrap and Agile. Check out my projects below to see what I've learned!!
